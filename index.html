About MyPlant Store

Since 2014, MyPlant has been a trusted plant store in Yogyakarta, committed to nurturing a green and vibrant community. As a purveyor of quality plants, we pride ourselves on offering an extensive range of botanical treasures that cater to every plant lover's desire. Whether you're seeking lush foliage for your living space or delightful blooms to brighten your garden, our knowledgeable team is dedicated to providing personalized recommendations and expert care tips. At MyPlant, we strive to foster a deep connection between people and nature, enabling our customers to create their own flourishing havens of serenity and beauty.
